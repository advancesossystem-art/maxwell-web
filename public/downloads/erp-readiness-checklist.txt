ERP READINESS CHECKLIST — Maxwell Electrodeal
==============================================
25-point assessment before your custom ERP project starts.
Download, print, and score each item (0 = not started, 1 = in progress, 2 = done).

EXECUTIVE & STRATEGY
□ 1. Executive sponsor named with budget authority
□ 2. Top 3 business outcomes defined (e.g. reduce stock-outs 30%)
□ 3. Go-live deadline tied to a business event (not arbitrary)
□ 4. Build vs buy decision documented with TCO over 3 years

PROCESS & OPERATIONS
□ 5. Core shop-floor / warehouse processes mapped end-to-end
□ 6. Inventory accuracy baseline measured (cycle count %)
□ 7. Production planning method documented (MRP, kanban, etc.)
□ 8. Quality rejection and rework workflow defined
□ 9. Purchase approval hierarchy documented

FINANCE & COMPLIANCE
□ 10. Chart of accounts aligned with ERP module structure
□ 11. GST / e-invoicing scope confirmed with CA
□ 12. Tally or existing accounting integration requirements listed
□ 13. Audit trail and user permission requirements defined

DATA & INTEGRATION
□ 14. Master data sources inventoried (items, customers, vendors)
□ 15. Data migration volume estimated (records per entity)
□ 16. Legacy system export format confirmed
□ 17. Third-party integrations listed (payment, logistics, BI)

PEOPLE & CHANGE
□ 18. Department champions assigned per module
□ 19. Training plan drafted (role-based, not one-size-fits-all)
□ 20. Parallel run period planned (old + new system overlap)
□ 21. Support SLA expectations set post go-live

TECHNICAL & VENDOR
□ 22. Hosting preference decided (cloud region, on-prem)
□ 23. Mobile / offline access requirements documented
□ 24. Security and NDA requirements shared with vendor
□ 25. Milestone payment schedule agreed before contract sign

SCORING
0–15: Not ready — invest 4–8 weeks in discovery first
16–35: Partially ready — schedule a readiness workshop
36–50: Ready — proceed to scoped estimate and timeline
